Who we are & what we aim

Cruzer is a project from an Ahmedabad based startup SocketMint, driven by group of young IT professionals working dedicatedly
to make “Vehicle Owning” a better experience for every individual.

Our aim is to provide a unique ecosystem for all vehicle owners where he can find solutions for all his vehicle related problems under one roof. We understand how precious a car or bike is for a travel freak. Now you can travel anywhere without fear, because Cruzer is there to take care of your precious! It is a platform where a vehicle owner can come and manage and track all his vehicle related statistics at his fingertips.

This is how we Cruze!!

Store

Store all vehicle related information on cloud, and access it from anywhere in the world. You'll get crucial statistics for your vehicle that will help you manage your vehicle better.

Share

Share this information between all your devices. Also share statistics and issues to trusted service stations, and they will respond with any suggestions and yummy offers.

Connect

Connect with your kind of people and be more awesome! Get the best rates and quality of service for your precious vehicle, by reading their feedbacks and ratings.
